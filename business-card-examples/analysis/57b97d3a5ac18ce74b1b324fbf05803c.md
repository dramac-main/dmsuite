## DESIGN DNA
- **Premium factors**: Clean whitespace, sophisticated color palette, architectural theme
- **Key design principles**:

  - Asymmetric balance with visual weight distribution
  - Consistent typography hierarchy
  - Professional restraint with single accent color
- **Color palette harmony**: Monochromatic gray base with single complementary orange accent
- **Mood/feeling**: Professional, trustworthy, modern, architectural expertise
- **Target audience**: High-end real estate clients, commercial property investors, luxury market consumers

The design conveys expertise in real estate through the architectural skyline motif while maintaining corporate professionalism through clean typography and restrained color usage.
