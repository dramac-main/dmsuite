## DESIGN DNA â€” What Makes This Work

### 1. The Diagonal as Brand Language
- The diagonal slash is **the** design element â€” it IS the layout
- Both sides use the same angle, mirrored â†’ **visual continuity** on card flip
- The diagonal creates natural **content zones** without boxes or borders
- **Angle (~18Â° from vertical)** is gentle enough to feel intentional without being extreme
- **Pixel-confirmed:** consistent 0.634 px/px slope across entire card height

### 2. Content Zone Strategy
- **Front dark zone:** Brand identity ONLY (logo mark + name + tagline)
- **Front light zone:** EMPTY (pixel-confirmed: zero elements detected)
- **Back light zone:** Functional contact details (address, phone, email, web, Skype)
- **Back dark zone:** Social presence (icon column) â€” secondary information
- Content assignment follows importance hierarchy mapped to visual weight

### 3. Typographic Discipline
- **Single font family** throughout (geometric sans-serif)
- **Only 3 weights used:** 700 (Bold/name), 400 (Regular/contacts), 300 (Light/tagline+title)
- **Letter-spacing is the secret weapon:** 0.02em â†’ 0.12em â†’ 0.20em â†’ 0.30em
- **Size hierarchy:** Name (5â€“5.5%) â†’ Contact/Title (2.5%) â†’ Tagline (2.2%)
- **Wide letter-spacing on tagline** creates elegance and air

### 4. Monochrome Power
- Only 2 background colors â€” dark charcoal + warm off-white
- **No accent color, no gradient, no pattern** (pixel-confirmed R=G=B everywhere)
- The warm off-white (`#F5F5F0`) prevents clinical coldness
- Text uses graduated grays: #FFFFFF â†’ #444444 â†’ #888888 â†’ #CCCCCC

### 5. Icon System
- **All icons:** thin circle outline + minimal glyph inside
- **Consistent diameter** across contact + social icons (~3.5% of H)
- **Contact icons** (back-light zone): gray `#888888` â€” functional
- **Social icons** (back-dark zone): white `#FFFFFF` â€” branded
- **Vertical rhythm:** Equal spacing (~8% of H)

### 6. Front/Back Mirror Relationship
- **Mirror structure:** Dark-left/Light-right (front) â†” Light-left/Dark-right (back)
- Creates **completeness** â€” card feels like two halves of one design
- When laid flat side-by-side, diagonals create an **hourglass/X pattern**
- Brand info (front) and contact info (back) occupy corresponding positions
