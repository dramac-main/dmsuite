## 8. DESIGN DNA â€” What Makes This Card Unique

### 1. Diagonal Corner Bracket Pair
Only **two** L-brackets â€” top-left and bottom-right â€” creating an implied diagonal axis. Not a four-corner frame. The asymmetry generates visual tension and movement.

### 2. Semantic Color Dots

- ðŸŸ  Orange â†’ Phone (warm, personal)
